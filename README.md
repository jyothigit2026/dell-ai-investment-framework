# Dell AI Investment Framework

**MSc Collaborative Industry Research Project with Dell Technologies**

## Overview

This repository showcases my MSc Collaborative Industry Research Project completed at **University College Cork (UCC)** in collaboration with **Dell Technologies**. The project focused on developing a structured decision-making framework to evaluate Artificial Intelligence (AI) investment opportunities using a multi-perspective business approach.

The research combines strategic business analysis techniques with decision-support methodologies to help organizations assess AI initiatives based on business value, technical feasibility, financial impact, and organizational readiness.

---

## Project Objectives

- Develop a structured framework for evaluating AI investment opportunities.
- Apply a multi-perspective decision-making approach.
- Support strategic AI investment decisions using quantitative and qualitative analysis.
- Improve transparency and consistency in AI investment evaluation.

---

## Business Problem

Organizations often struggle to prioritize AI investment opportunities due to multiple competing factors, including:

- Business value
- Technical feasibility
- Financial return
- Implementation complexity
- Organizational readiness
- Strategic alignment

This project proposes a framework that enables decision-makers to evaluate AI initiatives systematically.

---

## Methodology

The framework integrates established business analysis and decision-making techniques, including:

- Balanced Scorecard (BSC)
- Pugh Decision Matrix
- Multi-Criteria Decision Analysis (MCDA)
- Strategic Evaluation Framework
- Stakeholder Analysis
- Risk Assessment
- Decision Support Models
